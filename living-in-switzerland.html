Living in Switzerland

Oftentimes I get asked on advice regarding moving to Switzerland and I have difficulties giving an answer. I know people on both sides of the spectrum - I have met some who have grown fondly attached to the country and I have met others who are deeply dissatisfied with their stay. I believe that such type of a choice is highly personal and it is impossible to come to a decision just from anecdotal information about the country. What I would suggest is to take time to visit Switzerland, read about it and reflect on whether it makes sense to make a move.
